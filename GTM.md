# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM.md)

**SpendOptic** is a free self-serve lead-generation asset for **Credex**. Our GTM strategy focuses on hyper-targeted, zero-CAC channels where engineering leaders, fractional CTOs, and early-stage founders hang out right when they are feeling software subscription fatigue.

---

## 1. The Target Persona

- **The Core User**: Tech Lead, Engineering Manager (EM), or VP of Engineering at seed-to-Series A startups (5 to 30 people).
- **The Decision Maker**: The CTO or technical co-founder who holds the card and reviews monthly expenses.
- **Pre-intent Search Behaviors**: Right before discovering SpendOptic, this user is scrolling through their Expensify or Brex dashboards, sighing at recurring bills, or Googling search terms like:
  - *"Cursor Business vs Pro seats"*
  - *"Is ChatGPT Team plan worth it"*
  - *"How to set up Claude prompt caching api"*
  - *"startup credit cards with SaaS discounts"*

---

## 2. Where They Congregate Online

- **Subreddits**: `r/startups`, `r/SaaS`, `r/ExperiencedDevs`, `r/ycombinator`.
- **Communities**: Indie Hackers, YC Bookface (for active YC founders), Launch YC.
- **Slack/Discord Groups**: Python Discord (developer chat), local tech Slack groups (e.g. SF Devs, NY Tech), Dev.to slack.
- **Social Lists**: X (Twitter) lists of "Active AI builders", YC Founder directories, and HN "Show HN" launches.

---

## 3. The $0 Budget Playbook: First 100 Users in 30 Days

We will acquire our first 100 users without spending a single dollar on paid ads, using 4 highly tailored, organic distribution channels:

### Play 1: The "Brex Expense audit" Cold Outreach on X (Days 1–10)
- **Execution**: Target founders posting screenshots of their Breton/Expensify dashboards on X complaining about SaaS costs. Send a highly personalized, non-salesy DM:
  - *"Hey [Name], saw your tweet about SaaS fatigue. Built a free self-serve tool that audits AI subscription seat limits (min seat requirements on Claude Team/ChatGPT Team are a silent cash killer). It doesn't require OAuth or API keys. Scan your stack in 60s here: [link]. Let me know what savings it flags!"*
- **Target**: 30 DMs/day. Projected conversion: 15% click-to-audit rate = **45 users**.

### Play 2: The "Show HN" Value-First Launch (Day 12)
- **Execution**: Post SpendOptic as a "Show HN" on Hacker News. Write a transparent, developer-friendly intro:
  - *"Show HN: SpendOptic – Find wasted seats in your AI stack (Cursor, Claude, Copilot, APIs)"*
  - In the first comment, explain the mathematical rules openly (e.g. how Claude Team minimum of 5 seats drains $125/mo even for 2 developers, or why Cursor + Copilot is redundant). Developers respect open algorithms.
- **Target**: Top 20 on Show HN. Projected conversion = **60 users**.

### Play 3: Micro-Niche Subreddit Audits (Days 15–25)
- **Execution**: Monitor `r/startups` and `r/SaaS` for threads discussing *"reducing burn"* or *"startup tooling choices"*. Instead of saying *"use my tool,"* paste the manual audit breakdown of common setups (e.g. showing the math of switching to prompt caching) and drop the link as a reference: *"Calculated these plan thresholds on a tool I built to audit AI stacks, hope it helps."*
- **Target**: 5 targeted reddit threads. Projected conversion = **30 users**.

### Play 4: The Viral "CFO Screenshot" Loop
- **Execution**: When a user runs an audit flagging >$300/mo in savings, the UI prompts a highly noticeable option: *"Share this report with your CFO"*. Generating a share link creates a clean, anonymous public report `/share/[slug]` that strips company names and emails, making it fully safe to text or email.
- **Target**: 20% of high-savings audits shared. Projected conversion = **25 users**.

---

## 4. The Unfair Distribution Channel: The "Brex Partner Directory"

Credex sells discounted AI credits. By integrating **SpendOptic** directly into the partner perk dashboards of **Brex, Mercury, or Ramp**, we plug into an unfair distribution channel. 
Startups opening a Mercury account get flagged: *"Audit your software stack for free with SpendOptic and claim pre-approved Credex credits instantly."* This positions our audit tool exactly where financial decisions are made.
