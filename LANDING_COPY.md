## 3. High-Converting FAQ Block (5 Q&As)

### Q1: Is this tool actually free? What's the catch?
**Answer**: Yes, SpendOptic is 100% free and self-serve. We don't ask for your credit card or force you to sign in with GitHub. We build this as a value-first customer acquisition asset for **Credex**. Our audit engine helps you find wasted spend; for teams with significant savings, Credex is the solution to consolidate and purchase wholesale AI infrastructure credits (Cursor, Claude, OpenAI APIs) at a 20% flat discount.

### Q2: Do you require access to our Brex account, GitHub org, or API keys?
**Answer**: Absolutely not. We do not require any integrations, API tokens, or login authorizations. You simply input your current subscription parameters (seats, plans, API spends) manually in our rapid form. Your inputs are processed entirely in transient memory, and we strip all identifying credentials (company name, business emails) from our public shared reports to preserve total privacy.

### Q3: Why does Claude Team show such high savings for small teams?
**Answer**: Anthropic enforces a strict **minimum of 5 seats** to access the Claude Team plan. If you have only 2 or 3 active users but signed up for "Team," you are billed a flat $125/month. By downgrading your 2 or 3 seats to Claude Pro ($20/mo/user), you keep the same top-tier reasoning capabilities while saving up to $85/month instantly. Our audit engine automatically identifies and corrects this seat-minimum leak.

### Q4: How does the API credit pool discount work?
**Answer**: When startups call the Anthropic or OpenAI API directly, they pay standard retail pricing. Credex purchases massive API credit blocks from companies that pivoted or overforecasted their models. By routing your production API traffic through Credex's wholesale pools, you get the exact same tokens and endpoints while receiving a flat 20% discount on your monthly bill.

### Q5: What if my audit report shows "$0 in savings"?
**Answer**: We practice **Honest Auditing**. If you are already running an optimal AI software stack, our engine will tell you exactly that: *"Excellent work! Your startup is running a highly optimal AI software stack."* We will never manufacture fake plan optimizations to look smart. You can still sign up to get notified automatically the moment new pricing tiers, models, or credit packages apply to your stack.
